ADMISSION INFORMATION

Muhanga Technical Center (MTC) is a private TVET College located in the Nyamabuye sector of the Muhanga District, Southern Province. Its primary goal is to provide hands-on vocational training to prepare students for the labor market and self-employment.
Accredited Academic Programs
The school offers national certifications from Level 1 to Level 5 across various technical departments:
ICT & Software: Computer Systems Technology (L3), Software Development (L4-5), and Networking (L4-5).
automative Technology: Automotive Engine Technology (L1, 3) and Automobile Transmission/Electronic Systems (L4-5).
hospitality & Services: Culinary Arts (L1, 3-5), Food and Beverage Operations (L3, 5), and Hairdressing.
construction & Business: Masonry (L1, 3-5) and Accounting/Business Services (L3-5).


Campus & Facilities


Learning: The Muhanga Private Vocational Training Centre is equipped with dedicated workshops for its technical trades.
.Accessibility: The campus features wheelchair-accessible entrances and parking lots.

.Operating Hours: Typically open for administrative and academic services Monday through Friday, 08:00 AM – 05:00 PM.
.Admission & Enrollment
.Application Process: Interested students can apply online through the MTC Profile or by visiting the admissions office in Nyamabuye.

.Entry Requirements: Applicants generally need to provide academic transcripts from Senior 6 (S6) or equivalent levels, along with a national ID and recent passport photos.
