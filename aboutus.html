In Rwanda, MTC refers to the Muhanga Technical Center (MTC), which is a private Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) college. If your question is "What is MTC school in Rwanda not marketing?", the answer is Marketing itself. While it offers several business-related courses, "Marketing" is not listed as an accredited trade or department at the center. Accredited Trades at MTC Rwanda Instead of marketing, the school focuses on the following professional fields: ICT & Software: Software Development, Networking, and Computer Systems Technology. Engineering & Automotive: Automotive Engine Technology, Automobile Transmission, and Auto Electricity/Electronic Systems. Hospitality: Culinary Arts, and Food & Beverage Services. Business: Accounting (Levels 3, 4, & 5). Construction: Masonry. Services: Hairdressing and Aesthetics. Location & Contact The school is located in the Muhanga District of Rwanda's Southern Province.
