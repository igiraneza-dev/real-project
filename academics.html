ACADEMICS INFORMATION

Academics at Muhanga Technical Center (MTC) focus on practical,
competency-based vocational training across several technical trades. The institution is an accredited private TVET center providing certifications from Level 1 to Level 5.
Accredited Trades and Courses
The curriculum is divided into specialized departments designed for immediate labor market entry:


ICT and Software:


.Computer Systems Technology: Offered at Level 3.
.Software Development: Offered at Levels 4 and 5.
.Networking: Offered at Levels 4 and 5.
Automotive Technology:


.Automobile Engine Technology: Offered at Levels 1 and 3.
.Automobile Transmission and Control Systems: Offered at Levels 4 and 5.
.Auto Electricity and Electronic Systems: Offered at Levels 4 and 5.
Hospitality and Service:


.Culinary Arts: Offered at Levels 1, 3, 4, and 5.
.Food and Beverage Services: Offered at Levels 3 and 5.
Construction:


.Masonry: Offered at Levels 1, 3, 4, and 5.
.Hairdressing and Aesthetics:


A specialized one-year program often focused on Level 1 certification.
.Tailoring: Offered at Level 3.
Business:


.Accounting: Offered at Levels 3, 4, and 5.
.Academic Approach
.Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC): Training focuses on hands-on practical skills rather than just theory to ensure students are "job-ready" upon graduation.
.Certification Levels: Programs range from Certificate 1 (basic vocational skills) to Certificate 5 (advanced technical proficiency), which aligns with the national Rwanda TVET qualifications framework.
.Instructional Media: Students also have access to instructional video resources via the school’s digital presence to supplement classroom learning
